Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Key point: We do not collect, store, or have access to any immigration information you enter into the templates. Your personal data stays on your device.

What Data We Collect

Purchase Information (via Gumroad):

  • Email address (for delivery and updates)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Gumroad, not stored by us)
  • Purchase date and product purchased

Website Analytics:

  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time on site)
  • No personally identifiable information is tracked

What We Do NOT Collect

Your Immigration Data:

  • We do not collect names, addresses, or personal details you enter in templates
  • We do not store information about your K-1 petition or relationship
  • Web-based templates work locally in your browser—data is not transmitted to our servers
  • Downloaded PDF templates are processed entirely on your device

How We Use Your Email

We use your email address only for:

  • Delivering your purchased materials
  • Sending free updates when USCIS changes the I-129F form
  • Responding to support requests

We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Gumroad (Payment Processing):

All purchases are processed through Gumroad. They handle payment information securely and in compliance with industry standards. We receive only basic transaction details (email, purchase date, product). Review Gumroad's privacy policy at gumroad.com/privacy.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your immigration information, there's no centralized database of sensitive data. Your personal petition details exist only on your own devices and in the documents you create.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the email information we have on file
  • Request deletion of your email from our records
  • Opt out of update notifications (though we recommend staying informed about form changes)
